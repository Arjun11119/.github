Choosing the Right Format for Your Device

The movie’s file format impacts playback compatibility. Common options include:

  • MP4: Universally compatible and efficient in size.
  • MKV: High-quality video and audio but requires a specialized player.
  • AVI: Ideal for older systems but with larger file sizes.

Choose the format that suits your device and storage capacity.
